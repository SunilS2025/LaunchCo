Frequently Asked Questions

Common Questions Answered

How long does implementation take?

Most pharmaceutical organizations begin using Launch Copilot within 2-4 weeks. This timeline includes initial configuration, integration with your existing systems (SharePoint, CRM, data platforms), security review, and team onboarding. We work closely with your IT, compliance, and Launch Excellence teams to ensure a smooth, validated rollout.

Does Launch Copilot integrate with our existing tools?

Yes. Launch Copilot integrates with common pharmaceutical technology platforms including SharePoint, Google Drive, Veeva CRM, Salesforce, Microsoft 365, and major data warehouse systems. Our product roadmap includes deeper integrations with Veeva PromoMats, Jira, Zinc, and regulatory submission platforms. During your demo, we'll discuss your specific integration requirements and technical architecture.

How do you ensure data security and regulatory compliance?

Launch Copilot is built on SOC 2 Type II certified infrastructure with enterprise-grade security controls. We implement role-based access by function, market, and data sensitivity level; maintain complete audit trails for all actions; provide version history with rollback capabilities; and enforce export controls for confidential data. Our AI governance framework ensures human-in-the-loop oversight for critical decisions, with full transparency into reasoning and data sources.

Which therapeutic areas does Launch Copilot support?

Launch Copilot supports pharmaceutical launches across all major therapeutic areas including oncology, rare diseases, immunology, neurology, cardiovascular, respiratory, metabolic disorders, and more. The platform adapts its frameworks, success criteria, and guidance based on your specific therapeutic area characteristics, regulatory pathways, payer dynamics, and competitive landscape. We continuously expand our therapeutic area intelligence based on real-world launch outcomes.

Can we customize Launch Copilot for our organization?

Absolutely. Launch Copilot is designed to align with your organization's unique launch methodology, governance structure, templates, approval workflows, and terminology. We configure the platform to match your specific processes—from brand strategy frameworks to medical review protocols to cross-functional decision-making forums. Your Launch Excellence standards become the platform's benchmarks for quality and readiness.

What kind of support and training do you provide?

We provide comprehensive onboarding including role-specific training for launch team members, launch leads, and executives; detailed documentation and self-service resources; regular office hours with pharma launch experts; and dedicated customer success resources. Your team gains access to our knowledge base of pharmaceutical launch best practices, case studies from comparable products, and a community of launch professionals across the biopharma industry.
