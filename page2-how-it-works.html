The Four Modes

Deep Dive into Mode Capabilities

Each mode is purpose-built to solve specific pharmaceutical launch challenges with enterprise-grade intelligence and automation.

🧭

Guide Mode

Strategic Advisory AI

Your always-available strategic consultant embedded in the workflow—providing therapeutic area-specific frameworks, regulatory intelligence, and competitive insights exactly when you need them.

  • Therapeutic area launch frameworks with success criteria benchmarked against comparable products
  • Regulatory pathway guidance customized by region, indication, and approval stage
  • Competitive positioning strategies informed by real-world market intelligence
  • Real-time decision coaching for complex cross-functional trade-offs
  • Risk scenario modeling with pre-built mitigation playbooks
  • Best practice recommendations from 100+ successful pharmaceutical launches
⚡

Builder Mode

Accelerated Creation

Transform deliverable development from days to hours with AI-powered generation, therapeutic area-specific templates, and intelligent auto-population from your data sources.

  • Gold-standard templates from successful pharma launches in similar therapeutic areas
  • AI-generated medical education materials, MSL resources, and scientific communications
  • Payer value story development with HEOR evidence integration and budget impact modeling
  • HCP engagement plans tailored to specialty, practice setting, and prescribing behavior
  • Leadership briefings and executive narratives auto-generated from underlying workstream data
  • Instant market-specific adaptation for language, regulatory environment, and cultural context
✓

Reviewer Mode

Quality Assurance AI

Ensure excellence with comprehensive quality checks that validate deliverables against regulatory requirements, brand standards, and therapeutic area best practices—identifying gaps before they become issues.

  • Automated regulatory compliance verification for promotional materials and claims
  • Gap analysis against launch success criteria and therapeutic area benchmarks
  • Cross-market consistency checks ensuring harmonization without sacrificing local relevance
  • Scientific accuracy validation comparing content to approved labeling and clinical data
  • Risk flag identification with severity scoring and escalation recommendations
  • One-click remediation applying AI-generated improvements with full audit trail
⚙️

Orchestrator Mode

Enterprise Governance

Manage launch complexity at scale with enterprise program governance that tracks dependencies, surfaces risks early, and provides real-time visibility from individual tasks to portfolio performance.

  • Cross-functional dependency mapping showing critical path and integration points
  • Automated readiness dashboards with objective scoring by workstream and market
  • Early-warning indicators surfacing potential issues 3-4 weeks before milestones
  • Executive storyline generation synthesizing readiness, risks, and recovery options
  • Portfolio countdown views across all active launches with drill-down capability
  • Scenario planning tools enabling "what-if" analysis and resource reallocation
Launch Lifecycle

From Kickoff to Continuous Learning

Launch Copilot supports every phase of your pharmaceutical launch with intelligent automation, strategic guidance, and systematic knowledge capture.

1

Launch Kickoff & Strategic Planning

Select product archetype, therapeutic area, target indications, and launch markets. Launch Copilot proposes tailored launch playbook with phase-appropriate success criteria, regulatory pathway considerations, and cross-functional workstream structures based on comparable pharmaceutical launches in your therapeutic area. Initial risk assessment identifies potential challenges before detailed planning begins.

2

Cross-Functional Deliverable Development

Teams across medical affairs, commercial, market access, and operations generate launch materials using therapeutic area-specific templates. Builder Mode auto-populates content from clinical trial data, market research, regulatory submissions, and HEOR evidence. Medical education materials, payer value stories, HCP engagement plans, and patient support program designs are created with embedded compliance guidance and quality checks.

3

Quality Review & Compliance Validation

Reviewer Mode runs comprehensive gap and risk checks against therapeutic area success criteria, regulatory requirements, and internal brand standards. Scientific accuracy is validated against approved labeling. Promotional claims are checked for regulatory compliance. One-click remediation applies AI-generated improvements while maintaining human-in-the-loop approval for critical decisions. All changes are logged in complete audit trail.

4

Cross-Functional Orchestration & Risk Management

Orchestrator Mode tracks dependencies, ownership assignments, and milestone deadlines automatically across all workstreams. Early-warning indicators surface potential blockers—supply chain constraints, regulatory delays, payer access challenges—weeks before critical dates. Leadership readiness dashboards update in real-time as work progresses. Scenario planning capabilities enable rapid response to emerging risks.

5

Continuous Learning & Knowledge Capture

Launch assets, performance metrics, competitive intelligence, and post-launch insights automatically feed your Launch Center of Excellence. Best practices identified during execution are tagged and indexed. Templates are updated based on real-world outcomes. Lessons learned flow systematically into future launches, creating compounding institutional knowledge that strengthens with each product commercialization.
